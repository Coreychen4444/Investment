# 2026-03-17 Review — GTC Day Early Entry

## Summary
Corey avoided the more aggressive 2x SK Hynix idea, but still entered MU / AAOI / LITE too early around GTC. The long-term AI infra thesis was not the main problem. The problem was treating early-session strength as confirmation and using high-beta optics names as if they were hard-core event winners.

## Verified facts
- NVIDIA GTC keynote: 2:00–4:00 p.m. ET
- LITE (2026-03-16): open ~656, high ~672.99, low ~607.27, close ~624.84
- AAOI (2026-03-16): open ~100, high ~101.32, low ~92.90, close ~94.07

## Main mistake
- Bought before the market finished digesting the event
- Confused long-term thesis strengthening with same-day price continuation
- Used high-beta optics names as if they were the cleanest event expression

## Specific lesson
LITE later did not need to be panic-sold, but the original entry around ~638 was still structurally inferior to a later confirmed entry around ~600.

## Rules extracted
- Early strength on catalyst days is not confirmation.
- Separate core thesis beneficiaries from high-beta sentiment beneficiaries.
- Better confirmation at a worse-looking price is preferable to a prettier early entry.
