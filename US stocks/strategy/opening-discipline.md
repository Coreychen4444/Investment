# Opening Discipline

## Two hard rules
1. **Set the stop before the trade**
   - Define the stop threshold in advance (e.g. cost -5% / -10%)
   - Execute without hesitation if triggered
   - Record why the stop was hit for review

2. **No trading in the first 30 minutes after the open**
   - Observe premarket trend, volume, and order flow first
   - Wait for the market to settle before acting
   - Especially important for volatile names such as MU / SNDK / high-beta AI infra names

## Reminder
Before trading, ask:
- Stop level set?
- Has the first 30 minutes passed and has price behavior stabilized?

Corey’s own lessons from early-open reversals and bad execution in volatile names.
