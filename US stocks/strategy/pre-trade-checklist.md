# Pre-Trade Checklist

Before any trade, answer:
1. Is this confirmed, probe, or early/risky?
2. Is this a core expression or a high-beta sentiment expression?
3. Has the market confirmed, or am I self-confirming?
4. If I do not enter now, is the worst outcome just earning less?
5. If I am wrong, what proves it and where do I reduce/exit?
6. How many entries are allowed? (Never more than 2)
7. Am I making a good trade, or chasing a pretty trade?

If 2 or more answers are unclear, do not trade.
